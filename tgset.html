Why TGSET?

Academic Eligibility

Prerequisite for recruitment as **Assistant Professors** or Lecturers in Telangana universities and degree colleges.

Validity

The TGSET certificate has **lifetime validity** for recruitment in Telangana institutions.

Qualifications

Master's Degree with at least **55% marks** (50% for reserved categories). No upper age limit to apply.
